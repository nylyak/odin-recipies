Pie Crust

This pie crust recipe made with butter produces a consistent dough that bakes into a crisp and flaky pie crust every time. It's perfect for any pie.

If you're intimidated by making homemade pie dough, you've come to the right place. This flaky pie crust is perfect for beginner bakers and seasoned professionals â€” it'll quickly become a staple in your recipe box.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups unbleached white flour
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • Pinch of stevia powder
  • 1 stick of butter (frozer)
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 3 rounded tablespoods cold water
  1. Sift flour, salt, and stevia powder into food processor
  2. Place butter ona board and cut into about 16 pieces using a sharp knife
  3. Distribute butter over flour and pulse several times until butter is broken into pea sized piieces and is well distributed
  4. Beat egg yolks breifly with a fork, dribble over flour mixture, and pulse once or twice. (Butter should still be visible in pea-sized and seed-sized pieces)
  5. Have water ready. Turnn on processor and immediately pour water in.
  6. Stop processor at once
  7. Refrigerate dough for several hours before rolling out and baking

Avacado Toast

Avacado toast is creamy, crunchy, and immensely satisfying. The crisp toast combined with the smooth avacado is an amazing combination that makes a satisfying breakfast or snack

It is fast and easy to prepare, making it an excellent choice for those who want to prepare a fancy breakfast, but do not have a lot of time. There are also many different ways that you could make it that you will not get tired of this delicious dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (sourdough bread is particularly good!)
  • 1 ripe avacado
  • Pinch of salt
  • Garlic (optiona)
  • Any other seasonings or toppings you want 😁

Steps

  1. Scoop the avacado out of it's peel and mash it in a bowl with salt and minced garlic
  2. Toast the bread the way you like it
  3. Spread the avacado on the slices of bread
  4. Enjoy! 👅
